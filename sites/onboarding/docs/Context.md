# 1ClickCart Digital Commerce Platform

## System Description

**1ClickCart** is a digital commerce platform designed to enable businesses across Guyana to easily participate in online commerce.

The platform provides merchants with the tools required to:

- Sell products online
- Manage orders
- Process payments
- Interact with customers
- Manage store operations

The long-term goal of 1ClickCart is to provide a platform similar to **Shopify**, but designed specifically for the Guyanese market where many small and medium-sized businesses lack accessible e-commerce infrastructure.

The platform is being developed and operated by **TechSmart Inc.**

---

# Platform Infrastructure

## Core Commerce Engine

The 1ClickCart platform is built on top of the **Ordering.co** infrastructure.

Ordering provides the foundational commerce engine responsible for the following capabilities:

- Online ordering systems
- Product and menu management
- Customer checkout
- Order processing
- Delivery and pickup management
- Payment integration

Using Ordering allows the platform to support real-world commerce operations without building all e-commerce infrastructure from scratch.

# Business Onboarding System

## Purpose

The **Business Onboarding System** is responsible for registering and preparing merchants to operate on the 1ClickCart platform.

Instead of administrators manually configuring merchant accounts, businesses submit their information through a structured digital onboarding process.

The onboarding system then prepares and provisions the merchant account.

---

# Merchant Onboarding Workflow

The onboarding process follows a structured pipeline.

### Step 1 — Merchant Registration

Businesses submit their operational information through the onboarding system.

Submitted information includes:

- Business name
- Contact information
- Business location
- Operating hours
- Payment configuration
- Merchant operational preferences
- Additional configuration required by the platform

---

### Step 2 — Data Processing and Validation

After submission, the onboarding system performs validation on the merchant data.

Validation tasks include:

- Ensuring required fields are completed
- Verifying data formatting
- Structuring merchant data for compatibility with the Ordering platform

The validated data is then prepared for provisioning.

---

### Step 3 — Merchant Confirmation Email

Immediately after submission, the system sends an **automated confirmation email** to the business.

The confirmation email:

- Confirms that the application was successfully received
- Verifies contact details
- Informs the merchant that their application is under review
- Provides an overview of the onboarding process

This provides immediate feedback to the merchant.

---

### Step 4 — Merchant Review

The platform team reviews the submitted application to ensure:

- Business information is accurate
- Required data is complete
- The merchant meets platform requirements

If additional information is needed, the team contacts the business.

---

### Step 5 — Merchant Account Provisioning

Once approved, the onboarding system prepares the merchant account inside the platform infrastructure.

Provisioning tasks include:

- Generating merchant identifiers
- Preparing configuration data
- Structuring merchant information for the Ordering platform
- Completing required system integrations

---

### Step 6 — Merchant Account Access

After provisioning, the merchant receives a second email providing access to the platform.

This email contains:

- Merchant login credentials or activation link
- Instructions for accessing the merchant dashboard
- Initial setup guidance

---

### Step 7 — Store Customization

After logging into the merchant dashboard, the business can configure their store.

Customization options include:

- Adding products or menu items
- Setting pricing
- Uploading product images
- Configuring delivery or pickup options
- Setting operating hours
- Customizing store branding

This allows merchants to configure their storefront independently.

---

### Step 8 — Store Activation

Once store configuration is completed and verified, the merchant store becomes active.

The business can then begin accepting customer orders through the 1ClickCart platform.

# Strategic Role of the Onboarding System

The onboarding system acts as the **gateway to the entire 1ClickCart ecosystem**.

Every merchant entering the platform passes through this system.

This system ensures:

- Data consistency
- Merchant configuration accuracy
- Scalable merchant provisioning

As the platform grows, the onboarding system can evolve into a **merchant provisioning engine** responsible for integrating merchants across multiple services.

Potential integrations include:

- Payment providers
- Delivery and logistics systems
- Merchant analytics tools
- Customer engagement systems
